SHERLOCK HOLMES

The Golden Years

About Book


Author: Kim H. Krisco
Genre: Fiction, Mystery, Crime

Few books have the power to transport you back to childhood. For me, any book with Sherlock Holmes in it, does the trick. I remember borrowing books from friendsand reading them huddled in a corner of my bed. It was a perfect accompaniment to wintry nights. It still is.
This book contain five, totally new, Sherlock Holmes adventures, which took place after Holmes and Whatson believe they have gone into retirement. Of course, you and I know, such a notion is irrealizable for either of them. Indeed, some of their most remarkable, and dangerous, adventure await them.
While each story is a separate adventure, the five tales within this collection follow one another chronologically. Some characters move from one story to the next as well.

Therefore it is suggested that you read each of the stroies in the order they appear in the book.
If you are not already a Sherlock Holmes fan, it is hoped that these stories will encourage you to in the origional canon, as well as the many excellent stories and the books that are keeping the spirit of Holmes and Whatson alive in the world.

About The Author

KIM KRISCO, author of three books in leadeship, now follows in the footsteps of the master story teller Sir Arthur Conan Doyle by adding few new, and exciting, Sherlock Holmes adventures to the canon. He captures the voice and style of Doyle, as Holmes and Whatson fin themselves unravelling mysteries in, and around, pre- WWI London that, as Holmes put it,"appears to have taken on an unsavory European influence."
  In Sherlock Holmes - The Golden Years, kim Krisco breathes new life into the beloved "odd couple," revealing deeper insight into Holme's and Whatson's protean friendship that has become richer with age... and a bit "pukish."
